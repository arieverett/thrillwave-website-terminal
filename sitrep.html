[ 03A // ingest ]==================================================
01
▼▼▼
INGEST
  • audience
  • competitor
  • category
ingest is the listening phase. we collect audience behavior, competitor patterns, category language, and cultural timing before we touch a concept. the goal is to get out of our own heads. we want the room as it actually is, not the room a brand wishes it had.
[ 03B // synthesize ]==================================================
02
◇◆◇
SYNTHESIZE
  • patterns
  • trust gaps
  • blind spots
synthesize is where the pile becomes a point of view. we look for repetition, tension, trust gaps, emotional openings, and the ideas competitors are all circling but not owning. this is where a guiding line appears: the clearest story the brand can tell, to the right people, at the right moment.
[ 03C // execute ]==================================================
03
⚡⚡⚡
EXECUTE
  • best story
  • production plan
  • ground truth
execute is where intelligence becomes content. we use the signal from millions of facts, not committee opinions, to decide what to make, what to cut, what to say, and where the story should live. the final work should feel human, but it should not feel random. it should carry the weight of the room behind it.
